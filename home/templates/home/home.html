Welcome to Earth Science Outreach UK

On April 2nd, 2025, we proudly became a registered charity (CIO) in the UK, dedicated to making Earth Science accessible to all. Our mission is to bridge the gap between underserved communities and the wealth of knowledge within Earth Science institutions, fostering education, engagement, and a deeper connection to the natural world for those who might otherwise be excluded.

We are proud to have a dedicated team of volunteers and trustees, who, alongside our partners, are ready to launch exciting events, workshops, and field trips. Our goal is to help everyone understand the Earth’s dynamic changes in their local area and how these changes are connected to global environmental issues.

We are also committed to using the lessons of the past to protect our planet and preserve our natural heritage for future generations. By studying past environmental changes, we aim to better understand current climate challenges and the urgent need to address global warming.

If you would like to support our efforts or get involved, we’d love to hear from you.
