Welcome to Earth Science Outreach UK

On April 2nd, 2025, we proudly became a registered charity (CIO) in the UK, dedicated to making Earth Science accessible to all. Our mission is to bridge the gap between underserved communities and the wealth of knowledge within Earth Science institutions, fostering education, engagement, and a deeper connection to the natural world for those who might otherwise be excluded.
